Recipe

Chocolate cacke

"Here is a fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. It's the best receipe ever. You will love it." Enjoy!

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black chocolate: 200 g

Sugar: 150 g

Butter: 200 g

Flour: 80 g

Instructions

1

Mix chocolate and butter

2

Add some sugar and stir, stir, stir...

3

Mix with flour and have a great time with that

4

Bake in the oven and do this do that to get longer text and finally we get it. You see? Isn't it wonderful? What a great cake!
